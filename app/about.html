Karl is a gifted soccer player who left a promising professional career to pursue the excitment and thrills of the legal profession. After realizing that the law was not enough of a challenge, Karl tried his hand at starting and building high tech start ups and hasn't look back since.

Karl has more letters after his name than most people you know, having earned a JD from Stanford Law School, Masters of Science in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Austin and a Bachelor of Arts from Dartmouth College.
