What is LucidFog?

We started LucidFog to help early stage founders with problems that we never found help with when we were founders. In those early days, there are many lonely moments where you need an honest, trusted ear to listen to your challenges and provide you with clear and objective advice.

There are problems you cannot discuss with your investors, your co-founders or your employees. Those are the problems you can discuss with us, and trust that we will never speak of it to anyone else.

Together, we have helped over two dozen companies as investors/advisors/mentors and friends. If you think we can help you please contact us.
